Who Is Jala Hokes?
In few words, an intelligent young coder on a mission for success. Let's dive deeper into detail. O n this page we will dissect who Jala is in three separate categories, starting with...
  • WHERE SHE STARTED

    • Tech was always apart of my life in one way or another, even as a child I liked to take apart my toys to see how they glow or play music. My father who is an atm fixer has always taught my siblings and I about the different ways in which computer science can be used from taking apart PC's to breaking down the functions of the motherboard, I was interested in the mechanics behind each element of electronic firmware. Around age 13 I enrolled myself into CodeAcademy to learn more about the working of a webpage. Following my brief study of CodeAcademy I went into designing web pages for family & friends with small businesses. My new hobby unveiled my love of web design but it still wasn't enough, I loathed the creative limit of pre-structured web pages, I wanted to ingolf each page with creativity and uniqueness. Late 2020 I was introduced to the RoadToHire program via my aunt who saw my potential in coding that I myself was still unsure of but soon as I entered the program during gateway they gave us each the task of designing our own web page based on a place we like to travel to, finally I could unboundedly design a page giving off my creative touches, it was a wonderful experience that made my love for coding & webdesign even greater and now my future as a software engineer begins.

  • WHAT SHE'S ACHIEVED

Though rigorous & tiring at times, I felt that I had a point to prove to myself, that I could push through the challenges to better my skill and learn new techniques which was acquired rather quickly as we jumped into learning new languages such as HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React & MySQL. During this learning process we had many projects that challenged my mind to retain & formulate methods to better build webpages & apps, aswell as earning honorable certifications such as an Agile Project Management Certificate & a Growth-Driven Design Certificate.
